Privacy Policy

Welcome to our app. Your privacy is important to us. This privacy policy explains how your data is handled when using this app.

1. No Data Collection

This app does not collect any personal information. All data you enter, such as your monthly income, work hours, or item prices, is used solely on your device and is not sent anywhere. Please note that standard, non-personal data related to app performance, such as crash logs and diagnostics, may be automatically collected and sent to the respective platform provider (Google Play or Apple App Store) to help us maintain app stability. This data does not contain any of the personal or financial information you enter into the app.

2. Local Data Storage

All information you provide is stored locally on your device. The app processes this data on your device to help you calculate how many hours you need to work to afford items and make conscious spending decisions.

3. No Sharing With Third Parties

The app does not share, sell, or transmit your information to any third parties. Your data remains entirely private and under your control.

4. Deleting Your Data

You can delete all data at any time by clearing the app data through your device settings. Once cleared, all information entered in the app will be permanently removed.

By using this app, you agree to the practices described in this privacy policy.
